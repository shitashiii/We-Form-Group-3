Welcome Theresian's

The St. Theresas’s School of Novaliches was established by its founders, Gregorio, Sr. and Adoracion Roxas, as a modest response to the needs of the times. In their desire to give back to the community, they built a three-classroom learning center in January 1990 at the crossroad of Kingfisher and Skylark Streets, Novaliches, Quezon City.It initially started with a workforce of three teachers, three administrative personnel and thirty-eight students. Since then the school has grown in size and magnitude and in its quest for excellence in academics and other fields of endeavour. STSN was awarded full government recognition for pre-school on July 31, 1991; recognition for elementary level on April 6, 1993; and, recognition for the secondary level on June 16, 1997.
